# Agentic AI: A Comprehensive Guide

Welcome to the comprehensive guide on **Agentic AI**! This book will take you from foundational concepts to advanced implementation of intelligent agents that can reason, plan, and execute complex tasks autonomously.

## What is Agentic AI?

Agentic AI refers to artificial intelligence systems that possess autonomous capabilities to:
- **Perceive** their environment through observations
- **Reason** about situations and make decisions
- **Plan** sequences of actions to achieve goals
- **Execute** actions using tools and resources
- **Learn** from experiences and adapt behavior

Unlike traditional AI systems that respond to specific inputs, agentic AI systems operate proactively, pursuing objectives with minimal human intervention.
